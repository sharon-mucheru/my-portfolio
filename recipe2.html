Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium heat. Add eggs and scramble them. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil, then saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots and peas, and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Return scrambled eggs to the pan and mix.
  6. Drizzle sesame oil (if using) and garnish with green onions.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
