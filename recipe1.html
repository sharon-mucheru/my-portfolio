Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until soft.
  2. Add chicken, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and tomato sauce. Stir well and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add oregano and chili flakes. Let cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
